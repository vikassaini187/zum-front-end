ZUM WAS BORN OUT OF A WORKING MOM'S FIRST HAND EXPERIENCE OF RAISING TWO ACTIVE KIDS AND MANAGING A FAST-PACED TECH CAREER IN SILICON VALLEY.

RITU NARAYAN

Founder & CEO, Zum

Ritu’s Story

Ritu’s mission is to improve the lives of busy working mothers through technology. Through Zum, Ritu aims to create an online marketplace that will have the scale of eBay and the trust of LinkedIn to provide high quality services and products for families with active kids. Ritu envisions that this online marketplace will build a community of working women who are connected to trustworthy and reliable resources.
