We stand for trust and safety

At Zum 'trust and safety' is the most important aspect of our value system and deeply embedded in our people, technology and processes.

OUR DRIVERS (A.K.A. ZUMERS)

Background

100% of our Zumers have childcare experience and have stellar driving and care records.

Selection

Only 1 out of 5 applicants are selected to become a Zumer and they work hard to maintain the highest ratings.

Vetting

All Zumers pass National level FBI and Department of Justice background checks, DMV record and referral checks on an ongoing basis.

OUR TECHNOLOGY

Matchmaking

Our smart technology uses data to customize your driver pool to your preferences.

Notifications

We always keep you in the loop about ride or service progress just as if you were doing it.

Tracking

You can use our app to track the location of your kid, speed of car and ETA at all times.

OUR PROCESS

Safe handoffs

We ensure kids are safely picked up and handed off according to parent's directions.

Open communication

We make every effort to keep you updated with up to date and accurate information and are available 24x7 when you need to reach us.

Regular checks

We perform regular checks on our drivers’ performance and their vehicles and incorporate your feedback in our checks.
