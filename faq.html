Booking a Ride (12)
  • How do I book a ride?

    How do I book a ride?

    Zum is an App-based service, so download our iOS App or Android App to get started. Once you have established an account and created a member profile for your child in the App, you can book a ride using our flexible booking tool where you can select your riders, and pick-up/drop-off times and locations.

    You will be able to see the total ride mileage, approximate ride time and estimated cost as you configure your ride.

    We will send you an email confirmation once you have successfully booked a ride.

  • When will I receive a ride confirmation? Can I review the driver’s profile before pick up?

    When will I receive a ride confirmation? Can I review the driver’s profile before pick up?

    You will receive an email confirmation of your ride shortly after you have successfully booked your ride. We typically assign drivers to rides by 8 pm PST the day before your ride. Once the driver is assigned to your ride, you’ll receive a text message: “Zumer has been assigned to your ride”. You can log in to the App to see your driver details which includes the picture of the driver, picture of their car, their license plate number and a short bio about them.

  • How do I rebook a ride?

    How do I rebook a ride?

    You can rebook a ride within the App as follows:

    • From the main menu, select “Rides” and tap on the “Completed” rides tab
    • Select the ride you would like to rebook, swipe the ride to the left and click on “Re-book”.
  • How do I schedule a recurring ride to school?

    How do I schedule a recurring ride to school?

    You can make any ride recurring within the App by using our calendar function to easily select the days you wish to repeat the ride.

    If you are in the process of booking a new ride, you can make it recurring by pressing on the “Set Recurring Ride” pushbutton on the ride confirmation screen that appears at the end of the booking process.

    If you would like to make a future ride recurring:

    • From the main menu, select “Rides” and tap on the “Upcoming” tab
    • Select the ride you would like to repeat
    • Scroll down to the bottom of the screen to the Recurring Ride section and tap “Edit”. A calendar will pop up and you can then select your dates on the calendar.
    • Remember to save the dates on the calendar.

    If you would like to make a past ride recurring:

    • From the main menu, select “Rides” and tap on the “Completed” tab
    • Select the ride you would like to repeat and rebook this ride for the next upcoming date.
    • Once you confirm this ride, the App will give you the option of making this ride recurring. Click that and a calendar will pop up and you can then select your recurring dates on the calendar.
    • Remember to save the dates on the calendar.

    You will not be charged for recurring rides at the time of booking. We charge you as each ride is completed and only when your child has arrived at their drop off location safely.

    You can cancel or edit your recurring ride at any time before 7pm PST the previous day. Learn more about our cancellation policy. (insert the link to cancellation policy)

    If you book 20-30 rides per month, we have bulk pricing discounts available. Find out more.

  • Will I get different drivers for different rides?

    Will I get different drivers for different rides?

    We assign a dedicated, small pool of drivers to your family, rather than random drivers every day to pick up your children. We want to provide a pool of drivers who you and your family always feel comfortable communicating with and can rely on. Feel free to leave us feedback or preferences on the drivers assigned to your family.

    • Can I have the same driver for my recurring rides?

      Can I have the same driver for my recurring rides?

      We assign each family a small pool of drivers to ensure familiarity and continuity. Committing one dedicated driver in our experience is detrimental as drivers can have conflicting personal schedules and emergencies. Thus, to provide consistent service to your family, we rotate among a small pool of familiar drivers. Our matching algorithm recognizes which driver has completed the most rides for your family and automatically gives first preference to that driver to accept your ride assignment.

    • What happens if my driver is running late?

      What happens if my driver is running late?

      Our drivers are highly vetted and always mindful about being punctual. Our built-in App technology has a driver sign-in function to remind drivers to be prepared before their shift. We are committed to clear and open communication with our drivers. In situations where a driver is running late, the driver or the Zum operations team will call you to inform you of the delay. You can also chat or call our live ops team from the App to get status updates.

      There are occasions where the traffic is heavy on the way to a drop-off location. It is advisable to plan for a 10-15 mins buffer time for drop-off. You can track the entire ride through the App once your driver has been assigned to the ride.

      Text message notifications will be sent to you as the ride progresses.

      The entire ride can be tracked through the App.

    • Do I need to provide a booster seat?

      Do I need to provide a booster seat?

      No, you don’t have to provide your own booster seat. If your child requires a booster seat, you can specify this in your child’s member profile.

      • From the main menu, select “Manage Members” and select your child
      • Use the “Special Seat Request” field to specify a seat requirement

      All Zum cars provide one booster seat on-demand. When our drivers see that your child requires a booster seat, they’ll have a booster seat in the vehicle.

    • Can I leave instructions for drivers?

      Can I leave instructions for drivers?

      Yes. When you schedule a ride, you can add specific notes in the “Pickup instructions” or “Dropoff instructions” fields so our drivers can follow accordingly. We encourage you to include notes about the pick up/ drop off location. For example: “Address is for ABC High School. Please wait in the parking lot.” You will also have the option to indicate whether signing in or signing out is required for your child. If so, the driver will sign your child in or sign them out according to the facility’s procedure. We include a time of 5 minutes for these procedures. If you anticipate that signing in/out takes longer, please book an additional wait time in the App. You can book wait times in increments of 15 minutes.

Carpooling (5)
  • What is carpooling?

    What is carpooling?

    Here at Zum, carpooling refers to rides where children from multiple families ride together in the same vehicle. Each child can be picked up and dropped off at the same or different locations.

    Carpooling is a fun and affordable option for your kids to ride in the company of their friends.

  • How do I create a carpool with my neighbors?

    How do I create a carpool with my neighbors?

    In order for you or other families to create a carpool ride, you must share your child’s profile with the other carpool families. This will allow these other families to include your child in rides with their children. When other families share their children’s profiles with you, you will be able to include them in carpool rides with your own children.

    When you schedule a carpool ride, you will have the option of specifying how the payment will be split amongst the carpooling families.

    To allow other families to include your child in carpool rides they create:

    • From the main menu, select “Manage Members” and go to your child’s profile
    • At the bottom of the screen, press the “Share Member” button, then enter the email address of the parent with you wish to share your child’s profile.
    • A text message notification will be sent to the other parent so they can accept your child’s profile in the App.
    • Once your child’s profile has been accepted by the other parent, they can add your child to rides they schedule with their own children.

    To create a carpool ride and have other children ride with your own child:

    • Speak with your friends and make sure they share their children’s profiles with you in the App. They will need your email address in order to do this.
    • You will receive a text message notification when a family is sharing their child’s profile with you.
    • When you open the Zum app, you will see a notification at the top of the screen that a child’s profile has been shared with you. You can accept or reject the profile. Once you accept, the child’s profile will be added as a shared member and you can include that child in rides you schedule.
  • How many stops are there in a carpool?

    How many stops are there in a carpool?

    We are able to accommodate up to 5 children as part of the carpool in one car and accommodate their multiple pick ups and drop offs.

  • Can I track the carpool ride that my child is part of?

    Can I track the carpool ride that my child is part of?

    Yes, the primary contact for each member (rider) will receive ride notifications and be able to track the entire ride.

  • Can Zum find other families for my child to carpool with?

    Can Zum find other families for my child to carpool with?

    At this point, we require that families invite others and create their own carpools. In our experience, the best way to create a carpool is with your neighbors and friends or through school or activity center parent lists.

Our Drivers (3)
  • Who are Zum drivers?

    Who are Zum drivers?

    Zumers come from all walks of life, but are typically part-time professionals, students, nannies, stay-at-home moms, teachers and nurses.

  • What criteria do Zum drivers need to meet?

    What criteria do Zum drivers need to meet?

    Background
    100% of our Zumers have childcare experience and have stellar driving and care records.
    - At least 21 years old
    - At least 3 years of experience working with children
    - At least 3 years of driving experience with clean driving record
    - Own a 2009 or above four door car in a very good condition
    - College education and/or experience working in a professional setting

    Vetting
    Applicants go through a very thorough interview process (both phone and in-person interviews) covering their personality, attitude, reactions to situations and sense of purpose. We go through multiple checks, such as address history, sex offender records, driving and SSN validations for every driver applicant. All Zumers pass National level FBI and Department of Justice background checks, DMV record and referral checks on an ongoing basis.

    Vehicle inspection & Road-test
    Only Zum-approved vehicles are deployed for rides (which are at least a 2008 or newer four door car in very good condition). We have a strict policy to monitor regular maintenance and perform checks that proper inspections were completed. We conduct in-person road tests for every single candidate to make sure they drive safely while having your kids with them.

    Selection
    Only 1 out of 5 applicants are selected to become a Zumer and they work hard to maintain the highest ratings.

  • What vehicles do Zum drivers use?

    What vehicles do Zum drivers use?

    Zumers use their own vehicles, which must be a 2008 or newer four door car in very good condition.

    How much will my ride cost?

    Our ride price starts from $8 for carpool rides (per child for a one-way trip) and $16 for a single (non-carpool) ride. Childcare is available for $6 per 15 minute increment. The price of your ride depends on your exact location, time of day and other details. We recommend creating a ride in our App or use our Fare Estimator to view ride-specific price estimates .   • When will I be charged?

    When will I be charged?

    Unlike other ride services, we don't charge you when you book the ride. We'll only charge your credit card once your children have arrived safely at their destination.

  • What are the additional charges that might be applied to my trip?

    What are the additional charges that might be applied to my trip?

    We charge the price you see when you book the trip. We totally understand that traffic will unavoidably add time and distance to your trip. However, we don’t charge for that. The only additional charges that may be applied to your trip are tolls, airport surcharges, or additional wait time.

    Tolls: If your driver pays a toll during your trip or needs to pay a toll to return after drop-off, then a charge for the toll may be added to your fare. The toll charge may not reflect the amount actually paid but will be charged later when the driver reports the toll fee to us.

    Wait times: We request our drivers to be at the pickup location 5 mins early and stay at the drop off location 5 mins late just in case of emergencies. Our drivers will factor in 5-10 mins of wait time for your child so they can pack their belongings after classes are dismissed or make their way to the front of the building. However, if the wait time goes beyond this range, we will charge $6 for every 15 minute increment.
