Who can apply

Everyone can be a driver or a nanny but not every one can be a Zumer. We select 1 out of every 5 applicants. In order to be a Zumer, you must take pride in providing exceptional service.

IN ADDITION
YOU MUST BE:

- At least 21 years old
- At least 3 years of experience working with kids
- At least 3 years of driving experience with clean driving record
- Own a 2009 or above four door car in a very good condition
- College education and/or experience working in a professional setting

Process

Apply today and become part of our amazing Zumer Community!

INTERVIEW

Applicants go through a very thorough interview process covering their personality, attitude, reactions to situations and sense of purpose.

BACKGROUND CHECK

We go through multiple checks such as address history, sex offender records, driving or SSN validations for every driver applicant.

VEHICLE INSPECTION

Only Zum-approved vehicles are deployed for rides. We have a strict policy to monitor regular maintenance and perform checks that proper inspections were completed.

Zumers

Zumers are not like other third-party drivers. They are an integral part of the Zumer community, who are anchored in our company’s culture and driven by a wider sense of purpose. Most of our Zumers are hired through internal referrals, which helps us build in strict quality controls and a sense of belonging.

Zumers are respected members from our local community.
