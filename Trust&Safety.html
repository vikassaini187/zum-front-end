Drivers are at the heart of our service

All our drivers are vigorously vetted and held to the highest standards required for the transport and care of your children.

SCREENING CRITERIA

All potential drivers (a.k.a “Zumers”) must have childcare experience and outstanding driving and care records. Applicants must meet the following criteria before more detailed vetting takes place:

  • Be at least 21 years old
  • Possess at least 3 years of experience working with children
  • Possess at least 3 years of driving experience with a clean driving record
  • Own a 2008 or newer four door car in very good condition
  • Have a college education and/or experience working in a professional setting

VETTING

Screened applicants proceed to our phone and in-person interview process which assesses personality, attitude, reactions to situations and sense of purpose. We conduct multiple verifications to ensure each applicant passes these checks:

  • Address history and Social Security Number validations
  • National level FBI and Department of Justice background checks
  • Sex offender records
  • DMV and driving record validation
  • TrustLine certification
  • Referral checks
  • In-person driving test

VEHICLE INSPECTION

The last step in our hiring process is ensuring driver vehicles meet our exacting standards. All Zum vehicles:

  • Must be a 2008 or newer, four door car in very good condition
  • Pass a 20 point vehicle inspection
  • Undergo regular vehicle maintenance and inspections

Our technology enables real-time transparency every step of the way

Matching Algorithm

Our smart technology optimizes your preferences with a small pool of drivers assigned to your family, ensuring long-term familiarity and consistency.

Real Time Tracking

Use the App to track your rides from start to finish, from the moment your driver proceeds to the initial pick-up point to when your child is dropped off at their destination.

Live Notifications

Receive text notifications at each step of the ride once it begins. You’ll be notified of when the driver is enroute to pick up your child, when the driver is within 100 feet of the pickup point, confirmation that your child was picked up and also dropped off.
